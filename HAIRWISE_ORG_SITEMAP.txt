NOTES
===============================================

1. All legal pages (terms, privacy, support) are required for 
   Apple App Store and Google Play Store submissions.

2. The privacy policy must include:
   - What data is collected
   - How data is used
   - How to request data deletion
   - Contact information

3. Terms of Service must include:
   - Medical disclaimer (NOT a medical device)
   - AI limitations disclaimer
   - Subscription/refund policy (managed by Apple/Google)

4. Support page must provide:
   - Contact email
   - Data deletion request method
   - Response time expectation
